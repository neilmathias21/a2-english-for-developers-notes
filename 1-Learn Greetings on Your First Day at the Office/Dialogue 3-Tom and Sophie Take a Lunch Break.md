# A2 English for Developers  
## Topic: Learn Greetings on Your First Day at the Office  
### Dialogue 3 — Tom and Sophie Take a Lunch Break

---

## Situation Summary
Tom and Sophie talk about lunch plans. Tom is hungry and wants to know whether Sophie is eating in the office or going out. Sophie invites him to join and suggests a café nearby.

---

## Dialogue Transcript
**Tom:** Wow, I'm so hungry. Is it lunchtime?  
**Sophie:** Yes, it is.  
**Tom:** Are you eating here or are you going out?  
**Sophie:** Today, I'm going out.  
**Sophie:** I can show you some places around here. Are you interested?  
**Tom:** Of course. Any favorite lunch spot around here, Sophie?  
**Sophie:** I know a nice one. It's a café right down the street.  
**Sophie:** Is that okay for you?  
**Tom:** Sounds great.  
**Sophie:** Yeah, it's nice to have a break from the office.  
**Tom:** It is. Is the café within walking distance?  
**Sophie:** Well, it's not far.  
**Sophie:** Come on, we can go together.
